Mengyu Fu

I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Here is a link to my PhD dissertation. As a member of Computational Robotics Research Group led by Prof. Ron Alterovitz, I worked on inspection/motion planning for medical/surgical robots (e.g. steerable needles, concentric tube robots, and parallel surgical robots). I earned my bachelor and master degree from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT).
